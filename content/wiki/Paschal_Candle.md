Effects[]
---------


* Gives Isaac a candle familiar that grants a [Tears](/wiki/Tears "Tears") bonus of +0.4 for each room cleared in a row without taking damage, which stacks up to 5 times, represented by how brightly the flame burns. Starts at 1 stack, and resets to zero if damage is taken.


Notes[]
-------


* The Tears bonus can exceed the normal Tears cap.
* Any self-damage, such as from (Curse Room) [Curse Rooms](/wiki/Curse_Room "Curse Room"), (Sacrifice Room) [Sacrifice Rooms](/wiki/Sacrifice_Room "Sacrifice Room"), the [Bad Trip](/wiki/Bad_Trip "Bad Trip") pill, and self-damaging items such as [(IV Bag)](/wiki/IV_Bag "IV Bag") [IV Bag](/wiki/IV_Bag "IV Bag"), won't reset the bonus.


Synergies[]
-----------


* [(Holy Mantle)](/wiki/Holy_Mantle "Holy Mantle") [Holy Mantle](/wiki/Holy_Mantle "Holy Mantle") / any similar item: The flame is not snuffed out when damage is negated by the shield.


Interactions[]
--------------


* [(BFFS!)](/wiki/BFFS! "BFFS!") [BFFS!](/wiki/BFFS! "BFFS!"): The candle appears larger, but otherwise does not change.
* [(Paschal Candle)](/wiki/Paschal_Candle "Paschal Candle") Paschal Candle: No extra familiars or stat boosts are granted from copies.
