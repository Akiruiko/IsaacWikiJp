 # Vanishing Twin ![image](/image/Vanishing_Twin.png) 


Effect
--------


* Creates a tiny pool of [yellow creep](/wiki/Creep#Yellow_Creep "Creep"), damaging enemies that cross it.
	+ The puddle deals 8 damage per tick or 24 damage per second.
