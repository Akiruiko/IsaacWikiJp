Effects[]
---------


* Grants 5 [bombs](/wiki/Bombs "Bombs").
* Bombs home in on enemies.


Notes[]
-------


* This item can make bombing out of rooms difficult, as the bombs may slide towards enemies and away from the door.


Synergies[]
-----------

--------------
