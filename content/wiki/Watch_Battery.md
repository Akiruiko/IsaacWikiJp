 # Watch Battery ![image](/image/Watch_Battery.png) 

Effects
---------


* Increases the chance of a [Little Battery](/wiki/Little_Battery "Little Battery") pickup after clearing a room by 6.67%.
* Increases the chance of random pickups being a [Little Battery](/wiki/Little_Battery "Little Battery") by 2%.
* Has a 5% chance to add an extra charge to Isaac's activated item upon completing a room.


Interactions
--------------


* [![image](/image/AAA_Battery.png)](/wiki/AAA_Battery "AAA Battery") [AAA Battery](/wiki/AAA_Battery "AAA Battery"): Watch Battery triggering after clearing a room will also trigger AAA Battery, provided the activated item was missing exactly 3 charges prior to clearing the room.
